What is Money?

Money helps us buy things we want and need! It comes in different forms, like coins and bills.

Coins and Their Values

Each coin has a special value! A penny is worth 1 cent, a nickel is worth 5 cents, and a dime is worth 10 cents.

Counting Coins!

Let's count coins together! If you have one dime and two nickels, you have 20 cents total!
