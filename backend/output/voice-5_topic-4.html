What are Fractions?

Imagine you have a yummy pizza! A fraction is a part of something. It shows how many pieces you have out of the whole pizza. Like if you have one slice out of the whole pizza, that's a fraction!

Half a Pizza: 1/2

Uh oh! Someone cut the pizza in half! That means there are two equal pieces. One of those pieces is called 'one-half' or 1/2. You get one piece out of two!

Quarter Pizza: 1/4

Now the pizza is cut into four equal slices! Each slice is called 'one-quarter' or 1/4. You get one piece out of four!

Comparing Fractions: Hungry Yet?

Which piece is bigger, 1/2 or 1/4? Let's look! The more slices you cut, the smaller each slice gets. So 1/2 is bigger than 1/4!

Pizza Time! Your Turn!

I have a pizza cut into 8 slices. If you take one slice, what fraction of the pizza do you have? Tell me in the chat!
